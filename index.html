about the symposium

There has been a tremendous advancement of technology in the cyber physical canvas and one of the burgeoning and the most talked about topics is Internet of Things (IoT).


Today IoT is changing the rule of the business and its future. Its ubiquitous presence has already been felt in all the sectors. One of the traditionally stabilized sectors is Aerospace and Defense where there is a gigantic potential for IoT to be used. It would mesmerize the sector in its value addition on production line, supply chain, material handling, component design and manufacturing, flight operation and management and many more.
The IoT can completely disrupt the future of the aerospace and defence industry. It is, therefore, necessary to evangelize the future generation and enlighten the aerospace community to harness it for a greener world of tomorrow. Today an average data volume generated for an aircraft fitted with single engine during a 12-hour flight is 844 TB. The mining of this volume of data would startle and enable the decision maker for the future planes and engines in designing quiet, safer and high performing aircraft for civil and defence applications.
